# How to Reorient Parts in CHITUBOX?

**Step 1. Launch CHITUBOX software and import your part.**

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="launch CHITUBOX"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-reorient-parts-in-chitubox/launch-chitubox.jpg"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

**Step 2. Click "Orientation" button to oriente your part.**

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="orientation button"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-reorient-parts-in-chitubox/orientation-button.jpg"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

<Admonition type="tip">
	If you want to put one plane of the part on the build platform, tick
	"Flatten by face" TAB and click the needed plane to put it sticking on the
	build platform.
</Admonition>

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="flatten by face option"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-reorient-parts-in-chitubox/flatten-by-face-option.jpg"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="flatten by face"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-reorient-parts-in-chitubox/flatten-by-face.gif"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

**Step 3. There are two ways to reorient a part. Click the "Orientation" button, and click "up and down arrow" to gradually increase/decrease XYZ angle by 0.5Â°each time or directly enter the value in the input box.**

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="adjust the orientation by changing the value"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-reorient-parts-in-chitubox/adjust-the-orientation-by-changing-the-value.gif"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Also you can also directly click the "Orientation" button or double click the part to activate the rotation indicator line and reorient the part by moving the mouse in X (red rotation indicator line) /Y (green rotation indicator line) / Z (blue rotation indicator line) direction.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="reorient the part by moving the mouse in X/Y/Z direction"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-reorient-parts-in-chitubox/reorient-the-part-by-moving-the-mouse-in-xyz-direction.gif"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

**Step 4. If you have serveral parts, you can rotate them together in the same direction or rotate them as a whole.**

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="select all models"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-reorient-parts-in-chitubox/select-all-models.jpg"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Tick "Select All" TAB and they will be rotated together in the same direction.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="select all models and rotate them together"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-reorient-parts-in-chitubox/select-all-models-and-rotate-them-together.gif"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Tick "Combination Center" and they will be rotated as a whole.

		}
