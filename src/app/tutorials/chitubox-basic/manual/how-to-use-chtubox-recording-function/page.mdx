# How to Use CHTUBOX Recording Function?

Launch your CHITUBOX and find the "Screen Capture/Recording" button.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="screenshots and records"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-use-chtubox-recording-function/screenshots-and-records.jpg"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Click "Screen Capture/Recording" button and you can see three options: watermark, screen recording and capture.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="watermark, screen recording and capture"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-use-chtubox-recording-function/watermark-screen-recording-and-capture.jpg"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Under "Watermark" option, tick the boxes if you want to add watermark on your pic or GIF and input your information.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="watermark option"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-use-chtubox-recording-function/watermark-option.jpg"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Then those informations will be on your pic or GIF.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="watermark information on the screenshot"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-use-chtubox-recording-function/watermark-information-on-the-screenshot.jpg"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="watermark information on the GIF"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-use-chtubox-recording-function/watermark-information-on-the-gif.jpg"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Click "Screen recording" option and input your needed seconds and fps (the number of frames transmitted per second which is commonly referred to the number of frames of animation or video).

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="screen recording option"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-use-chtubox-recording-function/screen-recording-option.jpg"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Then click "Start(Ctrl+F1)" button or use "Ctrl+F1" shortcut key and you can see the countdown on the top right corner of your CHITUBOX.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="screen recording countdown"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-use-chtubox-recording-function/screen-recording-countdown.jpg"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

When the countdown is over, a window will be poped up automatically to save your GIF.

Shift to "Capture(Ctrl+Shift+A)" option and click "Start" button or use "Ctrl+F1" shortcut key to launch capture function.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="capture option"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-use-chtubox-recording-function/capture-option.jpg"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>

Select your capture area and use the tools to leave marks or notes on your picture.

<ImageContainer>
	<AntdImage
		alt="select a capture area"
		src={
			"/en/academy/images/docs/tutorials/chitubox-basic/manual/how-to-use-chtubox-recording-function/select-a-capture-area.jpg"
		}
	/>
</ImageContainer>
